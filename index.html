Predulive

Predulive Edutech Foundation's primary mission is to inspire, aware, educate, and employ by driving large-scale job creation through entrepreneurship, innovation, and skills development. The youth, especially, those living in rural areas are unaware of the new job opportunities. Predulive provides an opportunity to discover their capabilities in an encouraging environment.
